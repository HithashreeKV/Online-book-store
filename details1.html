Alice in the Wonderland

Author:Lewis Carroll

Price:₹ 1,237.00

Description: The story begins with Alice sitting by a riverbank with her sister. She spots a White Rabbit, who is in a hurry and muttering about being late. Intrigued, Alice follows the Rabbit down a rabbit hole and finds herself in a strange and bizarre place called Wonderland. In Wonderland, Alice encounters a series of strange characters and undergoes various surreal adventures. These include: The White Rabbit: Always in a rush, he serves as Alice's initial guide to Wonderland. The Cheshire Cat: A grinning cat who can appear and disappear at will, offering cryptic advice to Alice. The Mad Hatter and the March Hare: Both host an endless tea party and engage in nonsensical conversation. The Queen of Hearts: A tyrannical ruler who frequently shouts, "Off with their heads!" at the slightest provocation. The Caterpillar: A hookah-smoking caterpillar who gives Alice confusing advice and teaches her how to change her size by eating pieces of a mushroom. Alice navigates a series of puzzles and absurdities, including altering her size multiple times and engaging in a bizarre trial involving a stolen tart. Throughout her journey, she often questions the nature of reality and her own identity. The novel is celebrated for its imaginative and playful use of language, its dream-like atmosphere, and its satirical commentary on Victorian society and education. It has inspired numerous adaptations in various media, including films, television shows, and stage plays, and remains a beloved classic in children's literature.
